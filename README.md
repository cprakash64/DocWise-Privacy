# Privacy Policy for DocWise

**Last Updated: March 17, 2026**

DocWise was engineered from the ground up with a strict "zero-knowledge" privacy architecture. We believe your documents are your private property, and our technical design enforces that belief.

### 1. Zero Data Collection & Zero Cloud
DocWise does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data, documents, or usage metrics on any external servers. We do not have user accounts, we do not require a login, and we do not have a backend cloud database. 

### 2. 100% On-Device Processing (Edge AI)
All intelligent features—including Optical Character Recognition (OCR), document classification, and semantic vector search—are processed entirely locally on your device's hardware using offline machine learning models. Your documents never leave your phone.

### 3. Local Encryption (The Secure Vault)
Any documents you scan or import into DocWise are encrypted locally. The data is stored in a secure, encrypted SQLCipher database isolated within your device’s native application sandbox. 

### 4. Device Permissions (Camera & Photos)
DocWise requests access to your device's Camera and Photo Library strictly for the purpose of scanning or importing documents into your local encrypted vault. We do not access, analyze, or upload any photos outside of the specific files you manually choose to import.

### 5. Third-Party Services
Because DocWise operates entirely offline, we do not integrate with third-party tracking, advertising, or analytics SDKs. You are not being tracked.

### 6. Changes to This Policy
If our zero-knowledge architecture ever changes, we will update this privacy policy and require explicit user consent. However, our core mission is local-only privacy, and we intend to keep it that way.

**Contact:**
If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy or our local-first architecture, please contact the developer via the app's App Store or Google Play Store listing.
