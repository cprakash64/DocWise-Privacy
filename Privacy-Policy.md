# Privacy Policy for DocWise

**Effective Date:** April 5, 2026

### 1. Introduction
Welcome to DocWise ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your sensitive documents remain secure. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices, the permissions we request, and how your information is handled when you use the DocWise mobile application.

**The DocWise Promise:** DocWise is built on a "local-first" architecture. We do not upload, sync, or store your documents, extracted text, or vault secrets on our servers or any external cloud databases. 

### 2. Information We Process (Locally)
To provide our core features, DocWise processes certain information directly on your device hardware:
* **Images & Documents:** When you use the scanner or import a file, the image is processed locally. 
* **Extracted Text (OCR):** Text, dates, and financial figures extracted from your documents are parsed entirely on-device.
* **Vault Data:** Any custom categories, passwords, or credentials you save are stored in an encrypted database on your device.

**None of this data leaves your device.** We do not have access to your documents or the information contained within them.

### 3. Device Permissions We Request
DocWise requires specific device permissions to function. You can revoke these at any time in your device settings:
* **Camera:** Required to scan physical documents and receipts.
* **Storage / Photo Library:** Required to import existing images from your gallery or export generated PDF files to your device.
* **Notifications (Optional):** Required to schedule and deliver local, on-device alerts for document expiration dates or upcoming bills.
* **Biometrics (Optional):** Used exclusively to unlock your secure vault. Your biometric data (Face ID/Touch ID/Fingerprint) is handled by your device's native operating system; DocWise never accesses or stores your biometric templates.

### 4. Third-Party Services
While DocWise does not harvest or sell your data, we rely on standard, trusted third-party infrastructure to power specific features:
* **On-Device Machine Learning:** We use Google ML Kit to perform Optical Character Recognition (OCR). This processing happens locally on your device and does not send your images to Google's servers for analysis.
* **In-App Purchases:** If you choose to upgrade to DocWise Pro, your payment is processed securely through the Google Play Store. We do not collect, process, or store your credit card or payment details. Purchase validation tokens are cached locally to unlock premium features.

### 5. Data Security
Security is a core feature of DocWise. Documents and credentials stored in your secure vault are protected using AES-256 encryption. Encryption keys are managed securely via Android's hardware-backed Keystore or iOS's Secure Enclave. Because we do not hold your encryption keys, we cannot recover your data if you lose your device or perform a factory reset without a manual backup.

### 6. Children's Privacy
DocWise is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

### 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect new features or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
